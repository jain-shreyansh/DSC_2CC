FAQs


1. What is getincampus?
‘getincampus’ is a one stop platform to sell and buy all your used products at your ease. All you need to do is Post your used products for sale and you will find buyers in your campus only. And if you are a buyer looking for used products all you need to do is register and get in touch with the sellers. You can also take the privilege of giving things for free
2. Is it mandatory for me to register, to view and post Ads?
Yes, it is mandatory to register to post and view ads. This feature has been designed keeping in mind the safety and security of VITians and make sure that no outsider can spam you.
3. Is getincampus only for Vitians?
Yes, getincampus is only and exclusively for the use of Vitians.
4. Is getincampus for free?
Yes, getincampus is absolutely free to all VItians. Buying, selling comparing and contacting is available to you at no charge!
5. Will my personal information be kept anonymous?
Don’t worry your personal information is safe with us only your contact information will be displayed to verified users so that you can be contacted.
6. How to contact us?
For any customer support or help visit our Contact Us page. You can directly write us at getincampus@gmail.com
